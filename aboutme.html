Interests

Design

I have always enjoyed the design process of the projects I have worked on. These range from software project designs (UI/UX), to optimizing circuit design, to physical projects (such as building and designing a cardboard chair), the drawing of these designs have always been the highlight to each of my projects. Seeing a design come to life in physical/virtual form is what I enjoy most.

Hardware Architecture & Verification

One of my favourite courses in my program has been the Digital System Design courses (CPEN 211 and CPEN 311). Working with SystemVerilog and Assembly language has helped me understand computers at a level deeper than I had previously known. I enjoy solving and piecing together systems to form a working design. Modeling hardware programs using SystemVerilog and translating it physically onto my FPGA board has been one of the things I have enjoyed most within my university program.

Most notibly, I enjoy solving problems on platforms like LeetCode, and HDLBits to hone my skills in algorithms and design.
